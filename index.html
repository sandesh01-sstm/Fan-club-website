Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB)

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is one of the most popular and loved franchises in the IPL, known for its passionate fanbase and electrifying brand of cricket. Representing energy, excitement, and entertainment, RCB has been home to legends like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Chris Gayle. the team continues to captivate fans with thrilling performances and unforgettable moments. After long waiting 17 years and in 18 th season RCB finallay the winner of ipl . The iconic red and gold jersey, the roaring Chinnaswamy crowd, and a never-give-up spirit define the RCB legacy. RCB is more than a team .it's an emotion for millions.

Cricket South Africa (CSA)

The Proteas are one of the most respected and admired teams in international cricket, known for their fighting spirit and fearless style of play. With a rich history and a tradition of excellence, they have thrilled fans across the globe with their skill, intensity, and sportsmanship. The team has been home to legends like Jacques Kallis, AB de Villiers, Hashim Amla, Dale Steyn, and many more icons of the game. the Proteas have produced countless unforgettable moments that have shaped the sport. The green and gold jersey, the pride of a nation, and the roar of loyal supporters reflect the heart of South African cricket. Finally after 27 years CSA won icc trophy an gave us moment to remember, celebrate.. Through highs and lows, the Proteas remain a symbol of resilience, unity, and passion. More than just a team, the Proteas are a legacy—and a true emotion for millions.
